Whole wheat Pancake mix

Description

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make! It also makes good waffles.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2½ tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Whisk whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl
  2. Cook on a hot griddle and serve with your choice of toppings.
